1. **"Know what THEY know"** — retail investors feel structurally disadvantaged (6.1% annual underperformance vs S&P, Dalbar study). Insider buying data is the closest legal proxy to "what the smart money actually does."

3. **Speed is the value prop** — 25% of abnormal returns accrue in first 5 days (Jeng/Metrick/Zeckhauser, Harvard/Wharton). Real-time is not a feature — it's the entire reason to pay.

4. **Trust through transparency** — competitor landscape has dark patterns (Seeking Alpha BBB rating: F, Motley Fool hides cancel button). Being transparent = competitive advantage.

- Leverages: information asymmetry awareness

- Landing/pricing = infrastructure tone ("signal intelligence layer")

- Leverages: market-rhythm urgency (not fake scarcity)
- "127 Form 4 filings today. Your pipeline: empty."
- "Last week: 847 filings. 23 triggered high-conviction alerts."
- Urgency is INFORMATIONAL — based on real filing volume, not promotions

## Tone of Voice

### Rules (absolute, all pages)
- Every sentence contains a number, a date, a company name, a metric, or a verifiable fact. If it contains none, delete it
- Assumed competence: never define P/E, FCF, ROIC. The reader knows
- Zero emoji. Zero exclamation marks. Zero superlatives
- Short sentences. Period punctuation = authority
- Loss framing in problem sections. Gain framing in CTAs
- "Form 4" (never "Form Four"), "SEC EDGAR" (always caps), "$4.2B" (never "$4.2 billion")

### Banned phrases
"It's worth noting" — "In conclusion" — "Don't miss out" — "Game-changing" — "Revolutionary" — "AI-powered" (overused) — "It remains to be seen" — "Having said that" — "On the other hand" — "At the end of the day" — "We're excited to" — "We're thrilled" — "We believe" — "In today's market" — "Let's dive in" — "Stay tuned"

### Institutional language (use)
Intelligence, insight, signal, edge, conviction, thesis, exposure, allocation, alpha, methodology, framework, transparency, monitor, position

### Retail language (avoid)
Moon, rocket, gains, tips, picks, guru, secret, hidden gem, explosive, massive, incredible, easy, anyone can, FOMO, hot stock

## Page-by-Page Psychological Strategy

### PAGE 1 — HOMEPAGE (/)

**Job**: In 5 seconds: what it is, why it's different, what's in it for me. In 30 seconds: scroll to pricing.

**Section 1: Hero**
- Psychology: Assumed competence + information asymmetry
- Headline: max 8 words, anchor to specific temporal delta (not vague "everyone else")
- Subheadline: mechanism (Form 4 -> AI -> alert) + a number
- Trust line: "All data sourced directly from SEC EDGAR"
- Primary CTA: data-forward ("See Recent Insider Trades"), NOT "Start Free"
- Secondary CTA: educational ("How It Works")

**Section 2: Live Alert Feed**
- Psychology: Social proof through volume + implicit loss framing
- The 5 CEO transaction cards are the most powerful copy on the page
- "Data updated every 15 seconds" — keep
- Add daily filing volume context

**Section 3: How It Works**
- Psychology: Competence demonstration via specific numbers
- Step 1: scope number ("17,325+ companies. Every Form 3, 4, and 13D.")
- Step 2: the filter problem ("80% noise. Our model isolates the 20% that signal conviction.")
- Step 3: the temporal delta ("Filing to inbox: under 60 seconds. Average retail investor: 24-48 hours later.")

**Section 4: Why Insider Buying Matters**
- Psychology: Academic proof = credibility armor
- Each stat needs full citation: "Jeng, Metrick & Zeckhauser, Harvard/Wharton, 1999"

**Section 5: Alert Example**
- Psychology: Show, don't tell
- Keep the detailed alert card — it's the best copy on the page
- Add specific timestamp: "Alert sent: 47 seconds after SEC filing"

**Section 6: Key Metrics**
- Psychology: Infrastructure authority
- Keep $4.2B / 2,847 / 17,325+ — real numbers, specific = credible
- Consider adding 4th number: latency or uptime

**Section 7: Reports Preview**

**Section 9: Final CTA**

- "You'll know in 60 seconds" — keep
- Add filing volume number
- CTA: "Start Monitoring Free"

**Section 10: Footer**

- Disclaimer: 2 sentences max
- Remove "Institutional Grade Data" from copyright line
