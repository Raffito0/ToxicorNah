- Screenshots are **not used to train AI models**

- Analysis results (scores, soul types, insights) may be stored temporarily to allow you to retrieve your results within the app session

**Your responsibility**: You represent and warrant that you have the legal right and, where required by applicable law, the explicit consent of all individuals whose personal data appears in the content you upload. **We do not**:
- Cross-reference or attempt to identify the Analyzed Individuals whose messages appear in uploaded screenshots
- Store conversation content permanently after analysis is complete

- Share uploaded content with advertisers or data brokers
